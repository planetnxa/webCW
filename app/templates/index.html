About the Creative

Marie, also known as RHI, is a graphic designer. A creative genius if you will. Born and raised in London, she knew she was never destined for the life of a tech-fleece babe. Inspired by the "beauty" around her, she decided to develop her own aesthetic. Marie creates visual experiences, not just for the "girl boss CEOs" you see on Instagram. Her goal is to design and create visuals that inspire others to be their authentic selves. We hope her projects inspire you to create and exist as your authentic self
